## 7-Segment Flip-disc Clock & Thermometer & Hygrometer

### Clock Power Supply
> [!IMPORTANT]
> The clock requires a good quality external 12V (minimum 1.5A) power supply, 5.5mm diameter plug, 2.1mm center pin positive +.
